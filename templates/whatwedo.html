What We Do?


We are a group of a college students who have successfully designed a web application to produce the best-suited crops to farmers according to their needs and to stop the spreading of disease in their crops.


We have designed a Machine Learning Model to recommend the best-suited crops to the farmers according to their environmental and soil conditions . Also we added a second machine learning model that aims at detecting disease in the crops given a picture of the diseased leaf.
