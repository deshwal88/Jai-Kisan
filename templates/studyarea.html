Study Areas


We conducted our study in the areas of north-western U.P. and gathered some data. The areas included locals of Banar, Jhinjana, Tana, Kaswerwa Kalan, Badheo and other rural areas of Shamli. After analysing data we concluded to some major problems in the agriculture sector. The two of the major problems that came out were inability to detect the right disease in the crop and poor selection of crops provided specific weather and soil conditions. So, we arrived at a cost-free and efficient solution that would target those problems.
